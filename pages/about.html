About Me

I'm a bioinformatician bridging the gap between complex biological data and actionable insights. With a background in electronics engineering and biotechnology, I bring a unique perspective to computational biology challenges.

My Journey

2014-2018

Electronics Engineering Foundation

Started with BTech in Electronics and Communication at IEM Kolkata. Developed strong analytical and problem-solving skills through circuit design and signal processing.

2018

Transition to Bioinformatics

Bachelor's thesis on DNA sequence analysis and protein visualization sparked my interest in computational biology. Integrated NCBI BLAST and PDB databases for protein analysis.

2020-2022

Advanced Studies at IIT Guwahati

Pursued MTech in Biotechnology, achieving CGPA 8.75. Master's thesis focused on RNA-seq analysis and NMD pathway characterization. GATE Rank 127 in Biotechnology.

2022

Research Fellowship

Junior Research Fellow at IIT Guwahati, investigating traditional medicine efficacy using computational approaches. Developed hybrid proteomics-cheminformatics pipelines.

2023-Present

Leading Computational Research

Project Associate-II at CSIR-IGIB, leading multiple computational biology projects. Published in top journals, developed tools like TRIPinRNA and StickForStats.
