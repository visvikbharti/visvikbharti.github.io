Vision behind StickForStats: Unlike existing tools like GraphPad Prism or Origin, StickForStats aims to help researchers not only perform analyses but truly understand statistical concepts through interactive learning. The modular approach allows users to explore simulations, visualize parameter changes, and understand statistical nuances before making analytical decisions. This educational foundation will empower researchers to make well-informed choices based on a deeper understanding of statistical methods.
